Our Brief Account

RMS International School was founded by the philanthropist couple Dr.M Srinivas and Smt. Rathnamma.M.Srinivas in 2010.

The chairman of RMS International School, P.U College and Management studies Sri .M.Srinivas received a honorary doctorate from Indian Virtual Academy for Peace Education in 2018, in Chennai.

Dr.M.sirinvas the founder of R.M.S groups of institutions firmly believes knowledge is power, Information is liberating while Education is the premise of progress, in every society, in every family. And, his initiated educational institution R.M.S. group works to bring about this radical change.





We have the abitlity for perfection among student. Our high class ability to teach students in a strctured curriculam brings standardity in our school. We care about the knowledge of all the students in our school. With this standard curriculum students not only get their marks but also understand the concept.

We use high-tech devices for teaching the students the basics of this technological world We use structured syllabus to implement in the online curriculam. We make sure that the students only use the technology in a safe manner. We care about the safety of the children in the slectronic world, we make sure that the children doesn't use any other violent or unsafe technology

We also give the children the experience of outdoor activities We also have music, dance, robotics and various other activities. We make sure that the students also learn extra curricular activities We make sure that the academical and extra curricular activities of the student is well balanced
